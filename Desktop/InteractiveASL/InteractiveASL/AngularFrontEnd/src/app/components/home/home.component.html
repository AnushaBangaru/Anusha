Learn

With Interactive ASL you can easily learn numbers, alphabet and words. Once you have learnt them you can use the Practice and Test section to reinforce the acquired knowledge.

Practice

You can practice and reinforce your Knowledge. You will not be scored so take your time and try as many times as you want until you get the correct answer.

Test

Now is time to probe your knowledge and consolidate what you've learned. You will be scored and tracked in order to give you recommendations and monitor your learning.
